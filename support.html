Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a contract?

Go to the EasySign hub on the Home tab, tap "New Contract," select your deal and state, choose the contract type, add signers, fill in the contract details, and tap Create. The PDF is generated with all your data pre-filled.

Does RoomScan Pro work on my iPhone?

RoomScan Pro requires an iPhone with LiDAR (iPhone 12 Pro or newer). The 3D Floor Plan mode uses Apple's RoomPlan API, and Photo-Realistic mode uses ARKit mesh reconstruction.

Are electronic signatures legally binding?

Yes. EasySign signatures are legally binding under the E-SIGN Act (15 U.S.C. § 7001) and UETA. Each signature includes biometric authentication, ECDSA cryptographic verification, and a tamper-proof audit trail.

Can I import documents from other apps?

Yes. Use the Share Extension — in Safari, Chrome, Mail, Files, or any app, tap the Share button and select "Import to EasySign." You can also scan paper documents with the built-in document scanner.

How does the free trial work?

The free tier includes 5 contacts, 2 deals, and 3 showings per month. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited access to all features. You can cancel anytime.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions → EasyRealty → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro features remain active until the end of your billing period.

Is my data secure?

All data is stored on your device with iOS Data Protection. Signed documents use ECDSA P-256 cryptographic signatures. Private keys are stored in the iOS Keychain. No data is sent to external servers without your action.

How does the mileage tracker work?

Go to Profile → Tools → Mileage Tracker. Log trips with date, purpose, and miles. The app calculates your IRS deduction at $0.70/mile (2025 rate). Export reports for your tax preparer.

System Requirements

  • iOS 17.0 or later
  • iPhone (optimized for iPhone 12 and newer)
  • RoomScan Pro requires LiDAR (iPhone 12 Pro+)
  • Document scanning requires camera access
