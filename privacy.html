Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

EasyRealty ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide:

  • Contact information (names, emails, phone numbers) you enter for your CRM
  • Property details and deal information you create
  • Documents you create, scan, or import
  • Signatures captured through EasySign
  • 3D room scans captured through RoomScan Pro
  • Mileage trip logs

Data Stored On-Device:

  • All your data is stored locally on your device using iOS Data Protection
  • Private signing keys are stored in the iOS Keychain
  • No personal data is uploaded to external servers unless you explicitly share it (e.g., sharing a document via email or AirDrop)

Data We Do NOT Collect:

  • We do not collect analytics or usage tracking data
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not access your contacts without your explicit permission
  • We do not store your data on our servers

Third-Party Services

The app may connect to the following external services for specific features:

  • FRED API (Federal Reserve Economic Data) — for live mortgage interest rates. No personal data is sent.
  • Census Bureau API — for demographic and property data. No personal data is sent.
  • FEMA NFHL — for flood zone determination. Only coordinates are sent, no personal data.
  • Apple StoreKit — for subscription management. Handled entirely by Apple.

Camera and Sensors

  • Camera — used for document scanning and RoomScan Pro 3D scanning. Images are processed on-device only.
  • LiDAR — used for RoomScan Pro room measurement. Data stays on your device.
  • Face ID / Touch ID — used for EasySign biometric authentication. Biometric data is processed by iOS and never leaves the Secure Enclave.
  • Location — used optionally for property search and showing route optimization. Location data is not stored or transmitted.

Electronic Signatures

EasySign creates legally binding electronic signatures in compliance with the E-SIGN Act (15 U.S.C. § 7001) and UETA. Each signature includes:

  • ECDSA P-256 digital signature for tamper detection
  • SHA-256 document hash for integrity verification
  • Biometric authentication record (method only, not biometric data)
  • Hash-chained audit trail for chain of custody

All cryptographic keys are stored in the iOS Keychain. Private keys never leave your device.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. We do not retain any copies of your data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

EasyRealty is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
